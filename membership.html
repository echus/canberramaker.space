Membership

We love seeing new faces and would love for you to come and try out the space to see if it suits you. Your first visit is free.

After that, to continue using the space you can either buy a casual day use pass or become a member by paying a monthly fee.

Why become a member?

  • Get access to our workshop any time the space is open and use our tools to work on your own projects (see our Facilities page for what we have available)
  • Become part of our community, share knowledge, and get support from fellow makers
  • Help support Canberra’s only community-led, not-for-profit makerspace - all of our fees go towards paying rent and improving the space and its facilities for our members
  • All fee-paying members get voting rights and have a say in the future direction of the space
  • Regular members who have proven they can look after the space can apply to get 24/7 key access for an increased membership fee

* Concession memberships are for anyone unable to afford our standard membership fees. Email us for a concession signup link. We also accept volunteer work as contribution towards membership fees - please have a chat to us if interested.
